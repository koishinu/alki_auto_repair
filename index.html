Our story

From a wooden boardwalk to Avalon Way.

Alki Auto Repair started life in 1918 as Turner Shell and Service, right across from Alki Beach — back when the road was still a boardwalk made of wood and sand.

After a few evolutions, including years as a Chevron station, the shop became Alki Auto Repair. It came under its current ownership in 1998 and moved to its present home on Avalon Way in 2014.

Today, every technician on our floor is an ASE Master Certified technician, and we keep a state emissions specialist on hand — the same hands-on standard that's kept West Seattle driving for over a century.
